Friends Church Ruaraka

Friends Church (Quakers), Nairobi Yearly Meeting was established on 3rd May 1987. The organization is a constituent member of the Friends Church in Kenya (FCK). Our Head Quarters is located at Friends International Centre, Ngong Road, Nairobi.
